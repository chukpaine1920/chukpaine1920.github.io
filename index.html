Hello, I'm Chuck. Welcome to my site.

As a former Army veteran, I mastered the technical and leadership skills necessary to train and teach individuals about the PATRIOT Missile System. In my first two years, I earned the rank of Sergeant. In 2010 I was one of six instructors picked to train Poland's Air Force officers on our classified equipment which paved the way for America's Patriot to Poland missions still implemented today.

My main focus and dedication were to programming and networking within our systems. I plan to use these skills and knowledge combined with my current studies to dedicate myself to a strategic partnership role in the technology industry.
